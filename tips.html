Simple Everyday Actions

  • ✅ Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • ✅ Fix leaking taps as soon as you notice them.
  • ✅ Take shorter showers—5 minutes or less!
  • ✅ Collect rainwater for watering gardens.
  • ✅ Use full loads in dishwashers and washing machines.
  • ✅ Use a broom instead of a hose to clean driveways.
